Easy Rice

A classic, goes well with everything.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Slice the garlic;
  2. Heat the oil in a pan;
  3. Fry garlic in low heat;
  4. Add rice as soon as garlic starts to get golden;
  5. Add salt;
  6. Add water, until it covers the rice;
  7. Let it cook until the water has evaporated;
  8. Serve;
