Easy Bolognese Sauce

This sauce is great with pasta, but also goes into lasagnas and even rice!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Pelati tomatoes
  • Bacon
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 medium onion
  • Salt and pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • English sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the bacon into small pieces;
  2. Cut the onion and garlic cloves into small pieces;
  3. Add some oil to a pan and let it heat;
  4. Add the ground beef to the pan;
  5. After the beef starts to cook, add the bacon;
  6. When the beef is almost completly done, add the ornion and garlin, stir;
  7. Add salt and pepepr;
  8. Add some English sauce, stir;
  9. Add the pelati tomato and some tomato paste;
  10. Close the pan, let it cook for 20 minutes, add a little bit of water if needed;
  11. Ready.
