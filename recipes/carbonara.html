Easy Carbonara

Carbonara is a simple dish but delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (300g)
  • Bacon (100g)
  • Parmesan cheese (75g)
  • 3 egg yolks and 1 egg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the bacon into small pieces;
  2. Grate the cheese. The smaller the better.
  3. Start cooking the pasta;
  4. Fry the bacon while cooking the pasta;
  5. Break the whole egg and 3 yolks into a bowl;
  6. When the bacon is golden and crispy, add some of the pasta's cooking water; Let it cook some more;
  7. Turn off the heat;
  8. Mix the cheese with the eggs;
  9. Mix the pasta with the bacon, in the same pan as the bacon;
  10. Add the cheese and egges mixture to the pasta with bacon;
  11. Add pepper and more cheese.
